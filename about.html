Our Story

Rooted in history, growing for tomorrow.

Humble Beginnings

A Legacy of Care

Founded by a collective of passionate agronomists and heritage farmers, Seed Vault began as a small community project aimed at rescuing disappearing local varieties of corn and beans.

Today, we represent a global network of dedicated growers who share a single vision: protecting our food sovereignty through the preservation of living seeds.

Our Preservation Mission

We believe that every seed carries a story. Our mission is to ensure those stories continue to be told, planted, and harvested for generations to come. By cultivating rare and endangered heirloom seeds, we actively fight against agricultural monoculture.

Making a Tangible Impact

Global Network

Species Saved
